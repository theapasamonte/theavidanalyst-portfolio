# Ma. Atheagem Pasamonte | Business Analyst

**Turning vague ideas into developer-ready specs.**

I am a freelance Business Analyst who helps SaaS companies, startups, and small
agencies turn unclear requirements into clear, documented, testable specifications.
My background spans QA testing, business analysis, project management, and UI/UX
design, which means I write requirements that developers can build from and that
hold up under testing.

I work in plain language so both technical and non-technical stakeholders stay
aligned from the first requirement to the final test case.

---

## What I do

- **Requirements Documentation** — Business Requirements Documents, Functional
  Requirements, and User Stories that development teams can build from.
- **Process Mapping and SOPs** — Current and proposed process flows that show how
  work happens today and how it should happen after a change.
- **Business Analysis + User Acceptance Testing** — End-to-end coverage from
  requirement to test case, with traceability so nothing gets lost between what
  was asked for and what was built.
